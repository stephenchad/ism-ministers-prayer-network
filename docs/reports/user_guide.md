## 2. For Members: Your Personal Account

Creating a free account unlocks the interactive features of the community.

### Registration and Login
1.  **Register**: Click the "Register" button on the homepage. Fill in your details and submit the form.
2.  **Login**: Once registered, use the "Login" page to access your account with your email and password.

### Managing Your Profile
Once logged in, click on your name or profile picture to go to your profile dashboard. Here you can:
-   **Update Your Information**: Edit your name, designation, and mobile number.
-   **Change Your Profile Picture**: Upload a photo to personalize your account.
-   **Update Your Password**: Secure your account by changing your password.

## 3. For Members: Engaging with Prayer Groups

Groups are the core of our interactive community.

### Finding and Creating Groups
-   **Find a Group**: Go to the "Groups" directory to browse or search for groups. Click on any group to see its details and, if it's public, click "Join" to become a member.
-   **Create a Group**: From your account dashboard, select "Create Group." Fill out the form with your group's title, description, location, and other details.

### Your Groups Dashboard
-   **My Groups**: In your account, you can see a list of all the groups you have created or joined.

### Interacting Within a Group
Once you are a member of a group, you can access its private space.
-   **Group Discussions**: Participate in the group's private chat or discussion forum to communicate with other members.
-   **Group Content Sharing**:
    -   **Events**: Post events that are specific to your group.
    -   **Photos**: Share photos with your fellow group members.
    -   **Resources**: Upload and share documents, links, or other resources relevant to the group.
-   **Group Settings (for Group Creators)**: If you created the group, you can access the settings to edit group information, manage members, and post rules.

## 4. For Administrators: Managing the Website

A dedicated team of administrators has access to a secure admin panel to manage the platform. This includes managing users, all website content (news, events, resources), and overseeing all groups to ensure the community remains a safe and welcoming environment.

## 5. Need Help?

If you have any questions or encounter any issues, please use the "Contact Us" page to get in touch. We are here to support you.

We are so glad you are part of our community and hope this guide helps you make the most of your experience!
